February 6 and 7.It saw 166 budding programmers test their mettle in 4 domains, Machine learning in health care, Application Development for NGOs,E-commerce and Cybersecurity. The Hackathon was organized by the students under the guidance of their teachers.The first prize was bagged by the team of D.J Sangvi College.TSEC looks forward to hosting a plethora of events and honing the engineers of tomorrow.

CONVOCATION

It is rightly said that “The tassel is worth the hassle.” The 2018 batch of Thadomal Shahani Engineering College celebrated the annual Convocation Ceremony on the 9th of February, in presence of principal sir, Dr. G.T Thampi, our HODs, our faculties and chief guest Mr. Kishu Mansukhani. Following the formal programme, the Ph.D students, rank holders and toppers from all the departments were felicitated. The joy and satisfaction of crossing every hurdle and passing out with flying colors was irreplaceable and reflected in the faces of our seniors. With the conclusion of another chapter of their lives, Thadomal Shahani Engineering College and TSEC-MIC heartly congratulates the 2018 Graduates!

TREE PLANTATION

TSEC Green Army coordinator Dr. Arti Deshpande in association with NSS-TSEC and with the help of MCGM organised a Tree Plantation Drive on July 22nd, 2019 at Pali Hill Railway Colony, Carter Road, Bandra West. This event was glorified by the presence of Cabinet Minister Mr. Ashish Shellar, Corporator Ms. Swapna Mhatre, Asst. Commissioner H West MCGM Mr. Sharad Ughade, Principal of Thadomal Shahani Engineering College Dr. G. T. Thampi. Over 50 students, from different branches participated in the plantation drive. Also, the TSEC Drama Team performed a street play creating awareness about the adverse effects of deforestation and the need to grow more trees.s!

PROJECT EXPO

The opening of Ecell event line up was marked the by inauguration of the Incubation Center in Old Building by our principal, Dr G.T. Thampi, which was followed by the Project Expo. Every department of TSEC participated in the Expo with great enthusiasm and the hard work they put in their projects was truly reflected. Our budding engineers exhibited their talents in various fields ranging from hardware and software to biology and chemistry. These displays were seen by faculty members, judges as well as students, who took back inspiration for future Expos.
